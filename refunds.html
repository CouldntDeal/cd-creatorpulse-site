Refund & Cancellation Policy

Effective date: March 1, 2026  ·  CD-CreatorPulse, operated by Kaide Clarin  ·  support@cdcreatorpulse.com

This policy covers refunds and cancellations for all purchases made through the CD-CreatorPulse platform, including community member subscriptions and Pulselings in-game purchases. All payments are processed by Stripe. By making a purchase through CreatorPulse, you agree to this Refund & Cancellation Policy.

1. Community Member Subscriptions

1.1 How Subscriptions Work

Community member subscriptions are recurring payments made to individual creator servers through the /subscribe command. Subscriptions are billed on a recurring basis (monthly or as otherwise specified at checkout) via Stripe. Your subscribed role and access are granted immediately upon successful payment and removed automatically upon cancellation or payment failure.

1.2 Cancellation

You may cancel your subscription at any time through the Stripe customer portal. A link to manage your subscription can be requested by contacting support@cdcreatorpulse.com with your Discord User ID and the server name. Upon cancellation:

  • Your subscription will remain active until the end of the current billing period.
  • You will not be charged again after cancellation.
  • Your subscriber role and associated access in the Discord server will be removed automatically when the billing period ends.

1.3 Refunds on Subscriptions

Subscription payments are generally non-refundable once processed. We evaluate refund requests on a case-by-case basis under the following framework:

  • Duplicate charges: If you were charged more than once in error for the same subscription period, we will issue a full refund for the duplicate charge.
  • Immediate cancellation within 48 hours: If you cancel within 48 hours of your initial subscription payment and have not materially used any subscriber-gated benefits during that period, you may request a refund. Approval is at our discretion.
  • Service unavailability: If the Service experienced a documented outage lasting more than 72 consecutive hours during a paid billing period, you may be eligible for a prorated credit or refund for that period.
  • Unauthorized charges: If you believe you were charged without authorization, contact us immediately at support@cdcreatorpulse.com. We will investigate and, where confirmed, issue a full refund.

Subscription payments that do not fall into the categories above are not eligible for refund. Dissatisfaction with a creator's community, a creator leaving the platform, or a change in subscription benefits offered by a creator are not grounds for a platform-level refund. Disputes related to creator conduct should be raised with the creator directly or flagged to us at support@cdcreatorpulse.com.

1.4 Creator Departures

If a creator's server is removed from the CreatorPulse platform and you have an active subscription, your subscription will be cancelled immediately and you will not be charged again. Refunds for partial billing periods in this circumstance will be evaluated on a case-by-case basis.

2. Pulselings Name Change Tokens

2.1 What Name Change Tokens Are

Name change tokens are one-time-use virtual items purchased for real currency that allow you to rename your Pulselings creature. Each token grants one rename. Tokens are tied to your Discord User ID at the time of purchase.

2.2 Refund Eligibility

  • Used tokens: Name change tokens that have been redeemed (i.e., a rename has been performed) are non-refundable under any circumstances.
  • Unused tokens: If you purchased a token and have not yet used it, you may request a refund within 14 days of purchase by contacting support@cdcreatorpulse.com with your Discord User ID and Stripe payment confirmation. Refund approval for unused tokens within this window is at our discretion.
  • Unused tokens after 14 days: Unused tokens are not refundable after 14 days from the date of purchase.
  • Technical failure: If a token was deducted from your account but the rename failed due to a verified technical error on our part, we will restore the token or issue a refund at your preference.

3. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, email support@cdcreatorpulse.com with the following information:

  • Your Discord User ID
  • The name of the Discord server involved (if applicable)
  • The date of the charge
  • The amount charged
  • The reason for your refund request
  • Your Stripe payment confirmation or receipt (if available)

We will respond to refund requests within 5 business days. Approved refunds will be processed through Stripe and returned to the original payment method. Refund processing time depends on your card issuer but is typically 5–10 business days after approval.

4. Chargebacks

If you initiate a chargeback through your bank or card issuer without first contacting us, we reserve the right to suspend your access to the Service pending resolution of the dispute. We encourage you to contact us directly at support@cdcreatorpulse.com before initiating a chargeback, as we are typically able to resolve payment issues faster and more simply through direct communication.

5. Platform Service Fees (Creators)

Platform service fees paid by creators are subject to the terms of the applicable partnership agreement. If no partnership agreement is in place, platform fees are non-refundable.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Refund & Cancellation Policy from time to time. The effective date at the top of this page will be updated when changes are made. Your continued use of the Service after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated Policy.

7. Contact

For refund requests or questions about this policy, contact us at support@cdcreatorpulse.com.
