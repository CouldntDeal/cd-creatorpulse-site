Add the bot, go through a short web wizard, pick your add-ons. That's setup. After that — creatures hatch, your economy grows, subscriptions manage themselves, and your community has something to come back to every day.

Setup

What happens when you add CreatorPulse

1
Instant

Bot joins — a private setup channel appears

A private #creatorpulse-setup channel appears in your server, visible only to you and the bot. It contains a single button to start setup. Nothing else is created yet.

2
~3 minutes

Complete the web wizard — 4 steps

Click the setup link. You'll go through four steps: your creator name, your subscription tier names and prices, which add-ons you want, and optionally connecting your Stripe account. No Discord knowledge required.

3
~30 seconds

Provision runs — your server is configured

CreatorPulse builds the channels and structures you selected, assigns roles, sets up your Creator Dashboard, and starts the Pulselings economy. The setup channel deletes itself when it's done.

4
Done

Your community hatches their first creatures

The moment your members run /pulse, they get their creature. From that point the economy runs itself — no daily input from you required.

Subscriptions

Set it up once, earn continuously.

During setup you'll optionally connect a Stripe account and configure up to 3 subscriber tiers. After that, everything — payments, role assignment, cancellations — is automatic.

What happens when someone subscribes

They click /subscribe in your Discord → a Stripe Checkout opens → they pay → their tier role is assigned instantly → they can access tier-gated channels → done.

If they cancel → Stripe notifies CreatorPulse → role is revoked automatically → access removed. You never touch it manually.

Add-ons

Toggle what you want. Skip what you don't.

During setup (and any time after from your dashboard) you can toggle each add-on independently. Nothing is forced on you.

What CreatorPulse does on its own.

Once you're live, here's what's happening in the background without any input from you:

🔄

Role reconciliation

Every subscriber's role is checked continuously. Expired subscriptions lose access. New ones gain it instantly.

🛠️

Channel self-healing

If a managed channel gets accidentally deleted or misconfigured, CreatorPulse detects and repairs it.

📊

Dashboard updates

Your Creator Dashboard placard refreshes with live data — member count, subscriber numbers, resonance score.

🌐

Station sync

Your planet's stats, leaderboard position, and market orders stay synced with The Station in real time.
