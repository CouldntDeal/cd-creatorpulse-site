community lives in.

Subscriptions
Earn from your Discord.
Keep most of what you make.

CreatorPulse handles the entire subscription stack inside your Discord — Stripe payments, role management, tier access, and cancellations. You set it up once, it runs itself. 💳

Stripe-powered, direct to you

Your own Stripe account. Money goes directly to your bank. CreatorPulse takes a small platform fee on subscription revenue — no monthly fees.

Up to 3 tiers, your names

Member, VIP, Elite — or whatever you call them. You set the names, you set the prices. Minimum $0.99/month.

✅

Roles update automatically

Subscribe → role assigned instantly. Cancel → role removed automatically. No manual management. Ever.

🔒

Tier-gated channels

Lock channels behind subscriber tiers. Your VIPs get exclusive access. It enforces itself — you don't have to police it.

💰

Free to install. You pay only when you earn.

No subscription fee to use CreatorPulse. The platform takes a small cut of Discord subscription revenue only. If you don't monetize, it costs nothing. If you do, you're already making money.
