What it does
One bot. Three things done right.

CreatorPulse is not a toolbox you have to configure. It's an assistant that handles the hard parts of running a Discord community — automatically.

🏗️

Structure, handled

Clean channels, correct permissions, clear onboarding for every new member. Your server stays organized as you grow — without you touching a single setting.

🔐

Subscribers, automated

Members subscribe inside Discord. Roles apply instantly. Paid channels unlock. Access removes itself if they cancel. You never manually manage a subscriber again.

📣

Updates, calm

Go-live alerts, announcements, and pinned community info — all managed in place. No spam. No duplicate posts. Your community always knows what's happening.
