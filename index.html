Professional Subscription Infrastructure for Creator Communities

CD-CreatorPulse is a multi-tenant SaaS Discord bot designed for content creators. It provides structured subscription management, Stripe-powered revenue splits, community automation, and operational tooling.

What It Does

  • Stripe Checkout subscription handling
  • Stripe Connect revenue distribution
  • Discord role management automation
  • Live status detection & alerts
  • Server structure provisioning
  • Creator control panel & operational dashboards

Who It Is For

CD-CreatorPulse is built for professional content creators operating Discord communities alongside platforms such as TikTok, YouTube, Twitch, and Kick.

Monetization Model

The bot is free to install and operate. Revenue is processed through Stripe. A transparent, milestone-based revenue share model is applied via Stripe Connect.

Availability: Controlled rollout phase.
