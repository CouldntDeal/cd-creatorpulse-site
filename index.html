Built for serious creators

Professional Subscription Infrastructure for Creator Communities

CD-CreatorPulse is a multi-tenant SaaS Discord bot built for professional creators. It provides structured subscription management, Stripe-powered revenue distribution, community automation, and operational tooling.

What It Does

  • Stripe Checkout subscription handling
  • Stripe Connect revenue distribution
  • Automated Discord role management
  • Live detection and notification systems
  • Structured server provisioning
  • Operational dashboards and control panels

Who It Is For

Designed for professional content creators operating Discord communities alongside platforms such as TikTok, YouTube, Twitch, and Kick.
