FAQ

Clear answers. Calm operations.

CreatorPulse is designed to keep your Discord professional without adding noise. Here are the most common questions.

Does CreatorPulse read DMs?

No. CreatorPulse operates on server surfaces and configured channels only.

Will it spam my server?

No. The core pattern is pinned placards updated in place (edit, do not append).

Where do payments happen?

Payments are processed by Stripe. CreatorPulse integrates with Stripe for subscriptions.

Can I uninstall?

Yes. You can remove the bot at any time. Your server remains yours.
