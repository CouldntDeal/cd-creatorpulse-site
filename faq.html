Is CreatorPulse public?

Not yet. Right now it’s limited access while we validate stability in real communities. Once the platform is rollout-ready, it will be open to any Discord server.

What does it do first?

From the moment it’s invited, it begins helping. It guides the owner/admin through setup and provisions clean, bot-managed surfaces where needed.

Does it take over my server?

No. The creator stays in control. CreatorPulse focuses on structure and bot-managed surfaces, and avoids destructive changes to your community culture.

How does the platform sustain itself?

Through a partnership revenue share on subscription revenue. The split is designed to scale in your favor as you grow. See the Partnership page for the ladder and examples.
