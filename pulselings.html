🥚Hatching
Type /pulse in any CreatorPulse server. Your creature generates from your Discord ID — same creature, every server.
⚡Leveling up
Feed your creature to gain XP. Active community members naturally level faster. Higher levels unlock building access and crafting.
💀Decay
Neglected creatures slowly decay in stats. Your community's activity keeps them alive. It's a loop — engagement has stakes.
🎭Flare roles
Unlock cosmetic roles at milestone levels — displayed in Discord. Visible status for your most dedicated community members.
Planets
Your community builds a world.

Every server forms a shared planet through collective creature activity. Planets specialize into one of three archetypes — each with a distinct economy, aesthetic, and production chain.

🌿

Harvester

Raw resource production. Feedhouses, Gathering Posts, and Deep Veins supply the economy with base materials.

⚙️

Production

Refines raw materials into compounds and alloys. The industrial middle layer that makes everything else possible.

🏛️

Architecture

Produces the rarest outputs — Resonance Crystals. Required for Monument construction and endgame crafting.

🏗️Buildings
Members at high enough level can propose building construction. The community deposits resources to complete them. Completed buildings produce passively.
🗳️Governor elections
Every 7 days, members vote for a planetary governor. The governor controls tax rates, Cabinet roles, and can call economic events. Real community politics.
🔮Resonance
Planetary resonance is a live score reflecting your community's health and activity. It affects resource drop rates and building efficiency across the whole server.
🏆Monuments
Five globally unique mega-builds that can only be completed by one server ever. Their construction triggers galaxy-wide announcements. They leave a permanent Codex entry.
Economy
A real market. Real stakes.

Pulse Points (⚡P) are the galaxy currency. The economy has three active sinks, a labor-powered production system, and a live order book market — no artificial scarcity, no admin intervention needed.

The Signal Market
Albion-style order book. Cross-server trading.

List resources for sale at any price. Place buy orders. The market matches by price-time priority. OHLC charts track price history. Every CreatorPulse community trades on the same market.

⚒️Crafting
Raw → Refined → Compound → Artifact. A three-stage crafting chain produces increasingly valuable goods. High-level creatures unlock advanced recipes.
📦Hopper & Packaging
Automated packaging pipelines process raw materials in bulk. Governors can configure processing rates and distribution.
🏦Stockpile
Planets have a shared stockpile. Governors set tax rates on deposits. The tax feeds public building construction and community events.
🦹Theft
High-level creatures can attempt resource theft from rival planets. It's intentional emergent drama — the economy has conflict baked in.
