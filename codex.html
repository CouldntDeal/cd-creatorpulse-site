Entry I
The Creation Era

Before communities existed, Pulselings drifted through deep space — ancient, formless, searching. When they finally found the communities, something ignited.

Six titles mark the creatures who were there as it all began. They cannot be earned. They cannot be transferred. They can only be claimed by being first.

The first creature to hatch across all of known space was Pulsaren, born on the world that would become Sanctuary. Pulsaren carries the permanent title ✦ The First ✦ — a distinction that will exist for as long as the galaxy does.
