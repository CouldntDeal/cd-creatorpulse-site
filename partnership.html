Partnership philosophy

  • The platform earns only when you earn.
  • The platform always earns some percentage (never zero).
  • The platform share decreases as you grow.
  • Platform share never drops below 10% (max creator split is 90% / 10%).
  • Milestones are permanent once unlocked.
  • Your split does not fluctuate week to week.

This structure is designed to reward growth while keeping your revenue predictable.

How milestones unlock

Each milestone requires BOTH:

  • Active supporter count
  • A monthly gross revenue floor

Both conditions must be satisfied. This prevents manipulation through ultra-low pricing and keeps the ladder fair across different creator models.

Once a milestone is unlocked, it is permanent. There are no downgrades if supporters drop temporarily.

Stripe and payouts

CreatorPulse uses Stripe for subscriptions. For creators, this means:

  • Payments are processed by Stripe.
  • Your share is applied automatically based on your milestone.
  • Your share is paid to your connected Stripe account.
  • The platform share is applied automatically through Stripe.

For transparency, financial events are recorded in a deterministic ledger for milestone tracking and auditability. Stripe remains the canonical payment processor.

Pricing floors (fairness)

Creators can customize tier pricing, but the platform enforces minimum price floors to keep milestones fair and prevent manipulation.

  • Tier 1 floor: .99
  • Tier 2 floor: .99
  • Tier 3 floor: .99

This keeps the milestone ladder consistent across different communities and pricing strategies.

Transparency and uninstall

  • CreatorPulse does not read direct messages.
  • CreatorPulse operates on server surfaces and configured channels only.
  • Placards are updated in place to avoid server spam.

If you remove the bot, automation stops immediately and no new data is collected for your server. Financial records may be retained for accounting integrity and audit requirements.
