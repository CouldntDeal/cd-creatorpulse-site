Partnership

The more you grow,
the more you keep.

CreatorPulse can optionally manage paid Discord subscriptions for your community. If you choose to enable them, revenue is split transparently — and the split gets better as your community grows.

⚠ Important — Read This First

These are not your Twitch subscriptions, YouTube memberships, TikTok monetization, Patreon income, or any other external platform revenue. CreatorPulse has zero access to and takes zero share of your platform income. Revenue share applies only to optional Discord subscriptions you choose to enable inside your server through Stripe. If you never enable subscriptions, this page doesn't apply to you at all.

✓

CreatorPulse is always free.

The core bot — server structure, live alerts, dashboards, member onboarding, Pulselings, the full platform — costs nothing. There is no subscription, no trial, no credit card required. If you never enable Discord subscriptions for your community, this partnership page does not apply to you at all.

When does revenue share apply?

  • Only if you choose to enable paid Discord subscriptions through Stripe
  • Only on successful, completed subscription payments — partial or failed payments don't count
  • Stripe's own processing fees are handled by Stripe, separate from the revenue share
  • If you never enable subscriptions, revenue share never applies — the bot is free to use

How payments actually work

Payouts go directly to you. CreatorPulse uses Stripe Connect.

1
You connect a Stripe account

During onboarding you connect your own Stripe Express account. Your money goes directly to your bank account.

2
Members subscribe inside Discord

No external checkout. Members subscribe using the /subscribe command. Stripe handles payment securely.

3
Revenue splits automatically

Your share goes directly to your Stripe account. The platform fee is applied automatically based on your current milestone. You see exactly what you earn in your Creator Dashboard.

4
Milestones upgrade automatically

As your supporter count and revenue grow, your milestone is evaluated and upgraded automatically. You never need to request a better split.
