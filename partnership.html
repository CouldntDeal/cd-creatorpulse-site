Revenue share facts (optional).

CreatorPulse can optionally manage paid content inside your Discord using Stripe. This page explains the revenue share model for those optional Discord subscriptions.

Important: these are NOT Twitch subscriptions, YouTube memberships, TikTok monetization, Patreon, or any external platform revenue. CreatorPulse does not access or take a share of your platform income. Revenue share applies only to optional Discord subscriptions you enable inside your server.

When revenue share applies

  • Only if you enable paid Discord subscriptions through Stripe
  • Only on successful subscription payments
  • If you do not enable subscriptions, there is no revenue share

How milestones unlock

Milestones are hybrid. Each milestone requires BOTH:

  • A minimum number of active supporters
  • A minimum monthly gross revenue floor

Milestones are permanent once unlocked (no downgrades).

How revenue is measured

  • Only completed payments count toward milestones
  • Stripe processing fees are separate and handled by Stripe
