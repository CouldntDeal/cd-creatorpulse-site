PARTNERSHIP

A split that funds stability — and scales in your favor.

CreatorPulse is built to be a long-term assistant for your Discord — including subscription management. To keep the platform reliable (hosting, monitoring, support, and continued development), the platform sustains itself through a revenue share on subscription revenue.

The goal is simple: as you grow, the platform share goes down. This is a partnership — not a forever tax.

Why a split exists
  • Reliability costs money: hosting, monitoring, logs, and uptime.
  • Safe rollouts: canary + stable release channels to protect creators.
  • Support & iteration: onboarding improvements, fixes, and polish without spammy churn.

CreatorPulse is designed to feel like a calm assistant — not an experiment that breaks your community.

What you get as a partner
  • Subscription handling: roles, entitlements, and access management.
  • Bot-managed surfaces: pinned placards that update in place (no clutter).
  • Guided setup: the bot asks the right questions so it can help properly.
  • Respect for your server: structure without deleting your culture.
