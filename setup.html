SETUP

Safe setup inside Discord.

CreatorPulse asks a few simple questions so it can configure your server correctly. You don't need to understand roles or permissions - the goal is to remove Discord confusion.

What it will do

  • Create organized categories and channels where needed
  • Configure permissions safely
  • Set up onboarding surfaces for members
  • Prepare optional subscriber channels (if enabled)

What it will not do

  • Delete your existing channels
  • Randomly override unrelated roles
  • Spam announcements repeatedly
  • Touch your external platform revenue (Twitch/YouTube/TikTok/etc.)

If you enable subscriptions

These are optional Discord-based subscriptions through Stripe. They are separate from your platform monetization.

  • Members subscribe inside your Discord
  • Roles update automatically
  • Paid channels unlock automatically

Time required

  • Usually a few minutes to configure
  • After setup, CreatorPulse runs quietly
  • Your server stays organized as you grow
