Don’t have a Pulseling yet?
Join a CreatorPulse server and type /pet inspect in Discord. Your creature hatches automatically — no setup, no waiting. Every creature is unique to your account and stays with you across all servers.

How Pulselings work

Think of it like a community Tamagotchi. Your creature lives in the server, and everyone can interact with it.

🥚
Hatching your creature
Use /pet inspect for the first time and your creature hatches. It gets a name from a curated list of 325 names, and six traits are rolled randomly to determine its look and rarity. You can’t re-roll — what you get is yours forever.
📊
Three stats to manage
Your creature has Hunger, Happiness, and Health. Keep them all above 60% and you earn a 1.25× XP bonus on every action. Stats decay naturally when you’re away — hunger drops 8 points every 12 hours. Don’t ghost your creature.
🍖
Feed — every 8 hours
Feeding gives +25 Hunger, +5 Happiness, and 10 base XP. Your first action of each day also stacks a +25 XP daily bonus. Make feeding your daily habit — it’s the most consistent way to keep stats healthy.
🎮
Play — every 4 hours
Playing gives +20 Happiness and 15 base XP — the highest XP-per-action. It costs -5 Hunger though, so don’t play on an empty stomach. Play has the shortest cooldown because happiness is the fastest stat to decay.
💤
Rest — every 6 hours
Resting gives +30 Energy, +5 Health, and 8 base XP. Health is slow to rebuild but important — if it drops alongside hunger, your creature enters a poor wellness state and XP slows to a crawl.
🌿
Feed tokens & community feeding
You hold up to 5 feed tokens. Earn one per level-up, one per daily first action, and one whenever someone feeds your creature. Spend them to feed other people’s showcased creatures. They get +15 Hunger + 5 XP, you get the good vibes — and a token back when they return the favour.
Max XP tip: Keep all three stats above 60% for a 1.25× multiplier on every action. If wellness drops below 30%, XP falls to zero. Your first action each day stacks a flat +25 bonus XP on top — use Play for that action to get the highest combined total (15 base × 1.25 + 25 bonus = ~44 XP in one shot).

Leveling up

XP required per level scales with your level. Early levels are quick. Later levels need daily consistency.

  • Each level-up gives +1 feed token
  • Level 10 — guaranteed common hat drop
  • Level 50 — guaranteed uncommon hat drop
  • Level 100 — guaranteed rare hat drop
  • Levels 25, 50, 75, 100+ — mutation offer (upgrade one trait slot)

Hat drops

Starting at level 10, each level-up has a random chance to drop a cosmetic hat your creature can wear. Shown on your showcase card.

Duplicate hat? It converts to +50 XP instead. Never a wasted drop.

Showcasing in the community

Use /pet showcase to post your creature card to the server’s showcase channel. Your card shows your level, rarity, stats, age, and hat. Other members can click Feed to spend one of their tokens on your creature — boosting its hunger and XP without you having to do anything.

  • Showcase posts auto-expire after 23 hours
  • You can re-showcase every 24 hours
  • Being fed gives your creature +15 Hunger, +3 Happiness, +5 XP
  • You (the owner) also earn +1 feed token each time someone feeds you

Showcasing is the easiest way to get passive XP and tokens while you’re offline. The more the community feeds each other, the faster everyone levels.
