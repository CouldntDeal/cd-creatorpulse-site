What to expect

What happens when you add the bot.

1
Instant

Bot joins — a private setup channel appears

A private #creatorpulse-setup channel is created, visible only to you and the bot. It contains a single button: Start Setup. Nothing else in your server is touched.

2
~3 minutes

Click to start — four quick steps in a web wizard

The wizard walks you through four steps:

  1. Your creator name — what the bot calls you across your server
  2. Subscription tiers — name and price for up to 3 tiers (you can skip this)
  3. Add-ons — toggle Live Alerts on or off, and select your platforms
  4. Stripe Connect — optional, links your payout account (can be done later)
3
~30 seconds

Provision runs — your server is configured automatically

CreatorPulse builds the channels and structures you selected, creates subscriber roles, sets up your Creator Dashboard, and starts the Pulselings economy. A progress bar tracks it in real time. The setup channel deletes itself when it's done.

4
Done

Members run /pulse — creatures hatch, economy begins

That's it. From this point the economy runs itself. Resources drop, the planet grows, the Signal Market fills with orders, and your Creator Dashboard stays live with your stats. No daily input required.

Common questions before you add it.

Does it modify or delete my existing channels?

No. CreatorPulse only creates new channels in its own categories. It doesn't touch anything that already exists in your server.

What do I need to have ready?

Just your Discord server and admin permissions. Stripe is completely optional — you can connect it later from your Creator Dashboard at any time.

How long does setup take?

The web wizard takes about 3 minutes. Provisioning completes in roughly 30 seconds after you confirm. Your server is live the moment it finishes.

Will it send spam or ping my members?

No. CreatorPulse doesn't send announcements or notifications unless you use the Announcement button in your Creator Dashboard or enable Member Events during setup.

What if I only want the creature game — not subscriptions?

Totally fine. Pulselings and all the core features work without subscriptions. You can skip the Stripe step entirely and enable it later if you change your mind.

Something went wrong during setup — what do I do?

Any errors during onboarding are automatically flagged to the team. You can also reach us directly at support@cdcreatorpulse.com and we'll sort it out.
