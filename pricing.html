PRICING + REVENUE SPLIT

Free to install. Clear revenue sharing.

CreatorPulse is built to manage Discord so creators can focus on content. The bot is free to install. Monetization is handled through paid subscriptions processed by Stripe, with a transparent revenue share model.

What you pay

There is no install fee. If you choose to offer paid tiers to your community, payments run through Stripe. CreatorPulse takes a platform share based on your growth stage. The intent is simple: as creators grow, the creator share increases.

What we handle

  • Stripe Checkout + subscription flows
  • Role assignment and enforcement (highest tier wins)
  • Bot-managed dashboards and pinned updates (no spam)
  • Creator support intake at support@cdcreatorpulse.com

Revenue share ladder (milestone based)

This is the intended model: higher platform share for small communities, progressively lower platform share as you grow.

Example ladder (illustrative - subject to final rollout readiness):

  • Early stage: ~50% platform / 50% creator
  • Growing: steps down in milestones (platform share decreases)
  • Large communities: can reach ~10% platform / 90% creator

The final ladder and triggers will be documented here as it is finalized.
